During my internship at **Amazon Web Services Vietnam** through the **First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program from **April 17, 2026** to **July 30, 2026**, I had the opportunity to learn, practice, and apply the knowledge acquired at university to a real-world working environment.

The 12-week internship was divided into two clear phases:

* **Weeks 1–8:** Learning and practicing core AWS services (EC2, VPC, S3, RDS, Auto Scaling, CloudWatch, Lambda, DynamoDB, CloudFront, CloudFormation, CDK, KMS, Cognito, WAF, etc.).
* **Weeks 9–12:** Implementing the **SmartHospital P2TB** project — a hospital management system on AWS Serverless, building a React/Vite frontend, integrating VNPay/MoMo payments, and blockchain verification for medical records.

Additionally, I participated in **two FCAJ Community Day events**, which expanded my knowledge of AI, cloud, and agentic systems. Through this experience, I improved my skills in **frontend development**, **cloud architecture design**, **debugging**, **report writing**, and **teamwork**.

In terms of work ethic, I always strived to complete tasks according to the worklog, complied with FCAJ regulations, and actively exchanged with my mentor and fellow interns when facing technical difficulties.

### Strengths

* Built a solid AWS foundation and applied it to the real-world SmartHospital P2TB project.
* Learned new technologies quickly: React/Vite, DynamoDB single-table design, Cognito, CloudFront, payment gateway integration.
* Demonstrated a strong sense of responsibility, completing the worklog and deliverables on schedule.
* Open to feedback and iterative improvement (frontend–backend mapping fixes, CCCD/recordId sync, UI state handling).
* Actively participated in the FCAJ community and Community Day events.

### Areas for Improvement

* **Discipline and time management:** Need more detailed sprint planning and avoid cramming work at the end of the week.
* **Proactiveness:** Should ask mentors questions earlier instead of spending too long debugging alone.
* **Technical communication:** Need to practice presenting solutions and reporting progress more clearly, in both Vietnamese and English.
* **System architecture thinking:** Should deepen understanding of serverless design, security, and AWS cost optimization before deployment.
* **Testing:** Need to build the habit of writing test cases and testing earlier in the development lifecycle.

### Post-Internship Direction

I aim to continue developing as a **Cloud Engineer / Full-stack Developer**, combining AWS knowledge with real application building experience. The FCAJ internship provided a solid foundation to pursue this career path.
